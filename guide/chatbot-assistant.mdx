# Chatbot Assistant

CUCEIUbicate includes an intelligent AI-powered chatbot that can answer your questions about the campus, provide information, and assist you with navigation. This guide will help you get the most out of the chatbot assistant.

## Accessing the Chatbot

The chatbot is easily accessible from anywhere in the app:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From Navigation">
    Look for the **chat** or **robot** icon in the bottom navigation bar or floating button on the home screen.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="From Menu">
    Open the main menu and select **Chatbot** or **Asistente** option.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Chatbot Interface

When you open the chatbot, you'll see a beautiful interface with:

### Header

<Note>
  The header features a stunning blue gradient design with the CUCEI Assistant branding.
</Note>

- **Bot Avatar**: A friendly bot icon with a green "online" status indicator
- **Title**: "CUCEI Assistant"
- **Status**: "Siempre disponible" (Always Available) with a green dot
- **Menu Button**: Three dots for additional options

### Chat Area

- **Message History**: Scrollable conversation display
- **Your Messages**: Appear in blue gradient bubbles on the right
- **Bot Responses**: Appear in white cards on the left with the bot avatar
- **Timestamps**: Each message shows when it was sent
- **Read Receipts**: Your messages show a checkmark when delivered

### Input Area

At the bottom, you'll find a gradient input section:

- **Text Input Field**: "Escribe tu mensaje aquí..." (Write your message here...)
- **Send Button**: Blue gradient circle with a paper plane icon
- **Character Limit**: 1000 characters maximum

## First Time Use

### Welcome Screen

When you first open the chatbot, a welcome modal appears:

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Welcome Message">
    A beautiful animated screen greets you with:
    
    - A robot icon
    - "¡Hola! Soy tu asistente" (Hello! I'm your assistant)
    - "Estoy aquí para ayudarte" (I'm here to help you)
    - An animated bot character
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read Capabilities">
    The welcome screen explains:
    
    "Puedo ayudarte con información sobre CUCEI, horarios, eventos, ubicaciones y mucho más."
    
    (I can help you with information about CUCEI, schedules, events, locations, and much more.)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Chatting">
    Tap the blue **Comenzar conversación** (Start Conversation) button to:
    
    - Close the welcome screen
    - Begin chatting with the assistant
    - Mark the welcome as seen (won't show again)
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Initial Greeting

Once you start, the bot sends an automatic greeting:

<Note>
  "¡Hola! Soy el bot asistente de CUCEI. Estoy aquí para ayudarte con cualquier duda que tengas sobre el campus, horarios, eventos y más. ¿En qué puedo asistirte hoy?"
</Note>

## Chatting with the Assistant

<Steps>
  <Step title="Type Your Question">
    Tap the input field and type your message or question. The keyboard will appear automatically.
    
    Examples:
    - "¿Dónde está la biblioteca?"
    - "¿Cómo llego al Módulo E?"
    - "¿Qué eventos hay esta semana?"
    - "Horario de la cafetería"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Send Your Message">
    Tap the blue send button (paper plane icon). Your message will:
    
    - Appear in a blue gradient bubble on the right
    - Show a timestamp
    - Display a checkmark when received
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Response">
    While the bot processes your message, you'll see a typing indicator:
    
    - Animated bot avatar with a pulsing ring
    - Three bouncing dots showing the bot is "typing"
    - Blue gradient bubble background
    
    <Note>
      The typing animation is designed to be engaging and shows the bot is actively working on your response.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read the Response">
    The bot's response appears in a white card with:
    
    - "Asistente CUCEI" header
    - Full message text
    - Copy button to copy the response
    - Timestamp at the bottom
    
    Responses are formatted for easy reading with:
    - Line breaks for clarity
    - Clickable links (if any)
    - Rich text formatting
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Chat Features

### Copying Messages

Each bot message includes a copy button:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Copy Button">
    Look for the small copy icon in the top-right corner of any bot message card.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap to Copy">
    Tap the icon to copy the message text to your clipboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirmation">
    You'll see an alert: "Copiado - El mensaje ha sido copiado al portapapeles"
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Copied text can be pasted into notes, shared with friends, or saved for later reference.
</Note>

### Clickable Links

If the bot's response includes URLs or web links:

- Links appear in **blue** with underline
- Tap any link to open it in your device browser
- Links are automatically detected in bot responses

### Message History

Your conversation is automatically saved:

- All messages persist between sessions
- Scroll up to view earlier messages
- History loads when you reopen the chatbot
- Messages include timestamps for reference

<Warning>
  Your chat history is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, your conversation history will be lost.
</Warning>

## Chat Management

### Inactivity Detection

The chatbot monitors for inactivity to help manage conversations:

<Steps>
  <Step title="2-Minute Warning">
    If you haven't sent a message in **2 minutes**, a modal appears asking:
    
    **¿Aún estás ahí?**
    
    "No he recibido mensajes en un tiempo. ¿Quieres mantener nuestra conversación?"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Action">
    You have two options:
    
    **Continuar** (Green button with checkmark):
    - Keeps the conversation active
    - Resets the inactivity timer
    - Returns you to chatting
    
    **Limpiar chat** (Red button with trash icon):
    - Clears the entire conversation
    - Keeps only the welcome message
    - Gives you a fresh start
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Auto-Clear (3 Minutes)">
    If you don't respond to the warning within another minute (total **3 minutes** of inactivity), the chat is automatically cleared to the welcome message.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The inactivity timer only runs when the chatbot screen is active. If you navigate away, the timer pauses.
</Note>

### Manually Clearing History

If you want to start a fresh conversation at any time:

1. Wait for the inactivity modal to appear (2 minutes)
2. Tap **Limpiar chat**
3. Or close and reopen the app, then wait for the warning

<Warning>
  Clearing chat history is permanent and cannot be undone. Only the initial welcome message will remain.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

### Asking Effective Questions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Be Specific">
    Instead of "¿Dónde está?", ask "¿Dónde está la biblioteca?"
    
    Specific questions get specific answers!
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="One Topic at a Time">
    Ask one question per message for clearer responses:
    
    ✅ Good: "¿Dónde está el Módulo A?"
    
    ❌ Less ideal: "¿Dónde está el Módulo A, el B y qué hora es?"
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Use Keywords">
    Include important keywords like:
    - Building names (Módulo A, Biblioteca)
    - Facility types (comida, laboratorio, aula)
    - Actions (cómo llegar, ubicación, horario)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Follow Up">
    If an answer isn't clear, ask follow-up questions:
    - "¿Puedes ser más específico?"
    - "¿Hay otra forma de llegar?"
    - "Dame más detalles sobre..."
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### What to Ask

<Accordion title="Campus Navigation">
  ✅ Ask about:
  - Building locations
  - How to get from A to B
  - Where facilities are located
  - Campus entrances and exits
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Facilities & Services">
  ✅ Ask about:
  - Library hours and services
  - Food and dining options
  - Study spaces
  - Computer labs
  - Administrative offices
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="General Information">
  ✅ Ask about:
  - Campus events
  - Academic programs
  - Campus amenities
  - General CUCEI information
</Accordion>

### What Not to Ask

<Warning>
  The chatbot cannot help with:
  
  - Personal academic records or grades
  - Financial or payment issues
  - Official university business (use official channels)
  - Technical support for the app (use app support)
  - Complex administrative procedures
</Warning>

For these topics, contact the appropriate CUCEI office or department directly.

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Bot Not Responding">
  If the typing indicator appears but no response comes:
  
  - Check your internet connection
  - Wait a bit longer (responses may take 10-30 seconds)
  - Try closing and reopening the chatbot
  - Restart the app if the problem persists
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Error Messages">
  If you see "Lo siento, ha ocurrido un error. Por favor, intenta de nuevo más tarde":
  
  - The bot service may be temporarily unavailable
  - Check your internet connection
  - Try again in a few minutes
  - Report persistent errors to app support
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Unexpected Answers">
  If the bot's response doesn't match your question:
  
  - Rephrase your question more clearly
  - Break complex questions into simpler parts
  - Use more specific keywords
  - Try asking in a different way
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't Send Messages">
  If the send button is disabled (gray):
  
  - Make sure you've typed a message (not just spaces)
  - Check that you haven't exceeded 1000 characters
  - Ensure the previous message finished sending
  - Restart the app if the issue continues
</Accordion>

## Privacy & Data

<Note>
  Your conversations are processed to provide helpful responses, but personal identifying information is not stored long-term.
</Note>

### What's Collected

- Message content for generating responses
- Anonymous usage statistics
- Conversation history (stored locally on your device)

### What's Not Collected

- Personal information you don't voluntarily share
- Messages are not linked to your student account
- No data is sold to third parties
