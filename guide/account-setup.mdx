# Account Setup

Welcome to CUCEIUbicate! This guide will walk you through creating your account and logging in to access all the campus navigation features.

## Registration Process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Registration Screen">
    Open the CUCEIUbicate app and tap on the **¿No tienes cuenta? ¡Regístrate!** link at the bottom of the login screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Institutional Email">
    Provide your **institutional email** (@alumnos.udg.mx or @gmail.com). This email will be used to verify your identity and recover your account if needed.
    
    <Note>
      Only institutional emails from the University of Guadalajara (@alumnos.udg.mx) or Gmail accounts are accepted.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create a Strong Password">
    Enter a secure password that meets the following requirements:
    
    - At least **8 characters** long
    - Contains at least **1 uppercase letter**
    - Contains at least **1 number**
    - Contains at least **1 special symbol** (e.g., @, $, !, %, *, ?, &, ., etc.)
    
    <Warning>
      Make sure to confirm your password by entering it again in the "Confirmar contraseña" field. Both passwords must match exactly.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit Your Registration">
    Once all fields are completed correctly, tap the **Continuar** button. The app will validate your email to ensure it hasn't been registered before.
    
    If successful, you'll see a confirmation animation and be redirected to complete your profile.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Completing Your Profile

After successful registration, you'll need to complete your profile with additional information:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enter Personal Information">
    Fill in the following fields:
    
    - **Nombre** (First Name): Enter your first name using only letters and spaces
    - **Apellidos** (Last Names): Enter your last name(s) using only letters and spaces
    - **Nombre de Usuario** (Username): Choose a unique username (3-20 characters)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Student Code">
    Enter your **9-digit student code** (Código Estudiantil). This code uniquely identifies you as a CUCEI student.
    
    <Note>
      Your student code must be exactly 9 digits. Make sure to enter it correctly as it cannot be changed later.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Academic Program">
    Tap on **Seleccione una carrera** to open a list of available programs at CUCEI. Choose your degree program from options such as:
    
    - Ingeniería en Computación (INCO)
    - Ingeniería Industrial (INDU)
    - Ingeniería Química (INQU)
    - And many more...
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Tap the **Terminar** button to finalize your profile. Once completed, you'll see a celebration screen confirming your profile is ready!
    
    Your email will be automatically saved for quick login on your next visit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Logging In

Once your account is created, logging in is simple:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Login Screen">
    Launch the CUCEIUbicate app. If you just completed registration, your email will be pre-filled.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Your Credentials">
    - **Email**: Enter your registered institutional email
    - **Password**: Enter your password
    
    You can tap the eye icon to show/hide your password as you type.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log In">
    Tap the **Iniciar Sesión** button. If your credentials are correct, you'll see a welcome animation and be taken to the home screen.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If you just completed registration, your email will be automatically pre-filled with a green checkmark indicator for convenience.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Registration Issues

<Accordion title="Email Already Registered">
  If you see the error "Este correo electrónico ya se ha registrado", it means an account already exists with that email. Try logging in instead, or use a different email address.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Invalid Email Format">
  Ensure your email follows the correct format and uses an allowed domain (@alumnos.udg.mx or @gmail.com).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Passwords Don't Match">
  Double-check that both password fields contain the exact same password. The confirmation will fail if there's any difference.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Username Already Taken">
  If you see "Este usuario ya ha sido registrado", the username is already in use. Choose a different username.
</Accordion>

### Common Login Issues

<Accordion title="Incorrect Email or Password">
  If you see "Correo o contraseña incorrectos", verify that:
  - Your email is spelled correctly
  - Your password is entered correctly (check Caps Lock)
  - You're using the credentials you registered with
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Connection Error">
  If you see "Error al iniciar sesión. Intenta de nuevo", check your internet connection and try again.
</Accordion>
