# Route Finding

CUCEIUbicate includes a powerful route-finding feature that helps you navigate from one campus location to another. Get clear directions, view your path on the map, and save frequently used routes.

## Opening the Route Finder

There are several ways to access the route-finding interface:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="From Home Screen">
    Look for the **route** or **directions** icon in the top navigation bar or floating action button on the home screen.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="From Map">
    While viewing the map, tap the **search** or **route** button that appears on the screen.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="From Location Details">
    When viewing a location's details in the bottom sheet, look for a "Get Directions" option.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Finding a Route

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search Route Interface">
    Tap the route-finding button to open the **Buscar Ruta** (Search Route) modal. This slides up from the bottom with an animated entrance.
    
    <Note>
      The interface features a clean blue header with "Buscar Ruta" title and a close button (X).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Origin Location">
    In the **Origen** (Origin) field marked with an upward arrow icon:
    
    - Tap to activate the input
    - Start typing the name of your starting location
    - As you type, suggestions will appear below the field
    - Tap any suggestion to auto-complete
    
    <Note>
      The search is smart and matches building names, IDs, and even aliases (alternative names for locations).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Destination Location">
    In the **Destino** (Destination) field marked with a map marker icon:
    
    - Tap to activate the input
    - Type your destination location
    - Select from the auto-complete suggestions
    
    <Warning>
      Both origin and destination must be valid campus locations. If you enter an invalid location, you'll see an error: "El origen o destino no existen en el mapa."
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Swap Locations (Optional)">
    Need to reverse your route? Tap the **exchange icon** (⇄) button between the two input fields to instantly swap the origin and destination.
    
    This is useful when you want to find the return route without retyping.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search for Route">
    Once both fields are filled, tap the blue **Buscar** (Search) button with the search icon.
    
    The app will:
    - Validate both locations exist
    - Calculate the optimal route
    - Display the path on the map
    - Show step-by-step directions
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Route Suggestions

As you type in either field, the app provides intelligent suggestions:

### How Suggestions Work

- **Prefix Matching**: Suggestions appear for locations starting with your typed text
- **ID Matching**: Works with location IDs and codes
- **Alias Matching**: Finds locations by alternative names
- **Case Insensitive**: Typing is not case-sensitive

### Selecting a Suggestion

<Steps>
  <Step title="View Suggestions">
    A dropdown list appears below the active input field showing matching locations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap to Select">
    Tap any suggestion from the list to auto-fill that field with the exact location name.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Suggestions Disappear">
    Once selected, the suggestion list automatically closes so you can move to the next field.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding Route Results

After searching for a route, the app displays:

### Map Visualization

- **Highlighted Path**: Your route is drawn on the map with a visible line or markers
- **Origin Marker**: Shows where you're starting from
- **Destination Marker**: Shows where you're going
- **Auto-Zoom**: The map automatically zooms and centers to show the entire route

### Route Information

<Note>
  Route details may include distance, estimated walking time, and key waypoints along the path.
</Note>

- Visual walkthrough of the path
- Major landmarks or turns along the way
- Clear indication of building connections and walkways

## Search History

The route finder maintains a history of your recent searches for quick access:

### Viewing History

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate History Section">
    Below the search fields, you'll see **Historial de Búsqueda** (Search History).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Previous Routes">
    Scroll through the list showing your recent route searches in the format:
    
    **Origin - Destination**
    
    For example: "Módulo A - Biblioteca"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reuse a Previous Search">
    Tap any history item to instantly populate both the origin and destination fields with that route.
    
    Then tap **Buscar** to search again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Managing History

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Delete Single Item">
    To remove one history entry:
    
    1. Find the entry in the history list
    2. Tap the red **X** icon on the right side
    3. That entry will be immediately removed
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Clear All History">
    To delete all history at once:
    
    1. Scroll to the bottom of the history list
    2. Tap the red **Limpiar Historial** (Clear History) button
    3. All search history will be erased
    
    <Warning>
      Clearing history is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you want to delete all saved routes.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Route-Finding Tips

### Getting Accurate Results

<Note>
  For best results, use the auto-complete suggestions instead of typing full names manually.
</Note>

- **Start typing and wait** for suggestions to appear
- **Use building codes** when you know them (e.g., "A", "B", "E")
- **Search facility types** like "Biblioteca" or "Comida"
- **Be specific** - "Módulo A" is clearer than just "A"

### Common Routes to Save

<Accordion title="From Main Entrance to Your Classes">
  Save routes from each campus entrance to your most frequent classroom buildings:
  
  - Entrada Boulevard → Your Module
  - Entrada Olímpica → Your Module
  - Entrada Revolución → Your Module
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Between Classes">
  If you have back-to-back classes in different buildings, save those routes:
  
  - Módulo A → Módulo E
  - Módulo C → Biblioteca
  - Jobs → Módulo D
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="To Common Facilities">
  Keep these frequently-used routes handy:
  
  - Any Module → Biblioteca
  - Any Module → Cafeteria/Comida areas
  - Any Module → Auditorio Matute
</Accordion>

### Using Routes Efficiently

<Steps>
  <Step title="Preview Before Walking">
    Always review the route on the map before starting to walk. This helps you:
    
    - Identify the general direction
    - Spot any major landmarks
    - Understand the path layout
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Note Key Waypoints">
    Look for distinctive features along your route:
    
    - Building connections
    - Courtyards or plazas
    - Entrance points
    - Outdoor vs. indoor sections
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Frequently Used Routes">
    Routes you use often will appear in your history. Keep them saved by:
    
    - Not clearing your history unnecessarily
    - Re-searching them periodically to keep them at the top
    - Making a mental note of your top 5 routes
  </Step>
</Steps>

</Warning>

If you want to explore different ways to reach your destination:

- Try intermediate waypoints
- Manually navigate using the map
- Ask the chatbot for suggestions

## Troubleshooting Route Finding

<Accordion title="No Route Found">
  If the app can't find a route between two locations:
  
  - Verify both locations are correctly spelled
  - Check that both exist on the campus map
  - Try using auto-complete suggestions
  - Make sure you haven't entered the same location twice
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Route Seems Indirect">
  If your route looks longer than expected:
  
  - The app calculates walkable paths and may avoid obstacles
  - Check if there are building connections you're not aware of
  - Some routes must follow specific walkways for accessibility
  - Try searching the reverse route to compare
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Suggestions Not Appearing">
  If auto-complete doesn't show suggestions:
  
  - Make sure you've typed at least 1-2 characters
  - Check your typing for typos
  - Try using building codes or simpler terms
  - Ensure the location exists on campus
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="History Not Saving">
  If your search history isn't being saved:
  
  - Check app permissions for storage
  - Ensure you completed the search (not just typed)
  - Try restarting the app
  - Verify you haven't disabled history in settings (if available)
</Accordion>
