# Campus Navigation

CUCEIUbicate provides an interactive map of the CUCEI campus that helps you explore buildings, locate facilities, and understand the campus layout. This guide will show you how to use the map interface effectively.

## Understanding the Map Interface

The home screen features an interactive campus map that displays:

- **Buildings and Modules** (A, B, C, D, E, etc.)
- **Entrances** (Boulevard, Olímpica, Revolución)
- **Facilities** (Library, Cafeteria, Food areas)
- **Special Locations** (Matute Auditorium, Jobs building)
- **Walkways and Paths** between locations

<Note>
  The map uses an SVG-based rendering system that allows for smooth zooming and panning while maintaining crisp graphics.
</Note>

## Navigating the Map

The map interface supports several intuitive gestures:

### Zoom Controls

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Pinch to Zoom">
    Use two fingers to pinch in/out on the map to zoom in or out. This gives you detailed views of specific areas or an overview of the entire campus.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Double Tap">
    Double-tap on any area of the map to quickly zoom in on that specific location.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Zoom Buttons">
    Look for zoom in (+) and zoom out (-) buttons on the map interface for precise zoom control.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Panning and Moving

- **Drag the map** with one finger to move around and explore different areas
- The map will scroll smoothly in any direction
- Use panning to find buildings outside the current view

### Viewing Building Details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate a Building">
    Find the building or location you're interested in on the map. Buildings are clearly labeled with their names and identifiers (e.g., "Módulo A", "Biblioteca").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap to Select">
    Tap on any building or location marker to select it. The selected location will be highlighted.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Information">
    A bottom sheet will slide up from the bottom of the screen showing:
    
    - Location name and identifier
    - Building images (if available)
    - Floor plans (for multi-story buildings)
    - Additional details about facilities
    
    <Note>
      Many buildings include floor plans showing classrooms, laboratories, and other facilities on each level.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Floor Plans">
    For buildings with multiple floors, swipe left/right or use navigation buttons to switch between floor views:
    
    - **Primer Piso** (First Floor)
    - **Segundo Piso** (Second Floor)
    - Additional floors as applicable
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Close Details">
    Swipe down on the bottom sheet or tap the close button (X) to dismiss the information panel and return to the map.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  Use these entrance markers to orient yourself and plan your arrival at campus.
</Note>

## Search and Find Locations

The map interface includes search functionality to quickly locate specific places:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search">
    Look for the search icon (magnifying glass) or search bar at the top of the screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Location Name">
    Start typing the name of a building, facility, or area. The search supports:
    
    - Building names (e.g., "Módulo A")
    - Facility types (e.g., "Biblioteca", "Comida")
    - Location IDs
    - Aliases and alternative names
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Suggestions">
    As you type, a dropdown list will show matching locations. Tap any suggestion to select it.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate to Location">
    Once selected, the map will automatically center and zoom to show the chosen location, and the information panel will open.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Map Layers and Filters

<Warning>
  Advanced filtering and layer options may vary depending on app version. Check the map settings or menu for available options.

- **Default View**: Shows all buildings and facilities
- **Accessibility View**: Highlights accessible routes and facilities
- **Category Filters**: Show only specific types of locations (academic, dining, services)

## Navigation Tips

### Getting Oriented

- Use the **campus entrances** as reference points
- Major walkways and paths are clearly marked
- Building labels help you identify where you are

### Planning Your Visit

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Destination">
    Use search or browse the map to locate where you need to go.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Identify Nearby Landmarks">
    Note buildings or facilities near your destination to help with wayfinding.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Use Route Finding">
    For turn-by-turn directions, use the [route finding feature](/guide/route-finding) to generate a path.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Common Map Scenarios

<Accordion title="Finding a Classroom">
  1. Search for the building/module name
  2. View floor plans in the bottom sheet
  3. Locate your classroom on the appropriate floor
  4. Note the nearest entrance or stairway
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Locating Food Options">
  1. Look for food location icons on the map
  2. Or search "Comida" to see all dining options
  3. Tap each location to see photos and details
  4. Choose the closest or most convenient option
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Finding Study Spaces">
  1. Navigate to the Biblioteca (Library)
  2. View available areas and spaces
  3. Check for computer labs in academic modules
  4. Look for quiet outdoor seating areas marked on the map
</Accordion>

## Map Performance Tips

<Note>
  The map is optimized for smooth performance on most devices. Here are tips for the best experience:
</Note>

- Allow the map to fully load before heavy interaction
- Avoid excessive rapid zooming which may lag on older devices
- Close the bottom sheet when not needed for better map visibility
- Use search instead of panning long distances

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Map Not Loading">
  If the map appears blank or doesn't load:
  - Check your internet connection
  - Close and reopen the app
  - Ensure you have the latest app version
  - Try clearing the app cache (in device settings)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can't Find a Location">
  If you can't locate a specific building:
  - Try different search terms or variations
  - Check if the location is labeled with a code (A, B, C, etc.)
  - Use the route finding feature as an alternative
  - Ask the chatbot assistant for help
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Bottom Sheet Won't Open">
  If tapping a location doesn't show details:
  - Make sure you're tapping directly on the location marker
  - Try tapping again or in a slightly different spot
  - Restart the app if the issue persists
</Accordion>
