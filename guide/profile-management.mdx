# Profile Management

Your profile in CUCEIUbicate displays your personal information, academic details, and allows you to customize your avatar. This guide will show you how to manage and personalize your profile.

## Accessing Your Profile

To view your profile:

1. From the home screen, tap the **Profile** or **Menu** icon (typically in the bottom navigation)
2. Your profile screen will display with all your information

## Profile Overview

Your profile screen displays the following sections:

### Header Section

<Note>
  The header features a beautiful blue gradient background with your avatar prominently displayed at the center.
</Note>

In the header, you'll see:

- **Your Avatar**: A customizable profile picture
- **Full Name**: Your first name and last name(s)
- **Username**: Your unique @username
- **Status Badge**: Shows "Activo" (Active) with a green checkmark

### University Information Card

This card displays your academic affiliation:

- **Universidad de Guadalajara** - Your university
- **CUCEI** - Your university center (Centro Universitario de Ciencias Exactas e Ingenierías)
- **Current Academic Cycle** - The current school year (e.g., 2025)

### Personal Information Section

The "Mi Información" (My Information) section shows:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Username">
    Your unique username prefixed with **@** symbol that identifies you in the system.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Student Code">
    Your 9-digit student code (Código Estudiantil) that uniquely identifies you at CUCEI.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Academic Program">
    Your degree program (e.g., Ingeniería en Computación, Ingeniería Industrial, etc.).
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Institutional Email">
    The email address you used to register and log in.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Each piece of information is displayed in a clean card with a colored icon:
- 🔵 Blue for Username
- 🟣 Purple for Student Code
- 🟣 Violet for Academic Program
- 🔵 Cyan for Email

## Customizing Your Avatar

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Avatar Selection">
    On your profile screen, tap the **camera icon** in the bottom-right corner of your avatar image.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Available Avatars">
    A modal will appear showing a grid of animal-themed avatar options including:
    
    - León (Lion)
    - Abeja (Bee)
    - Águila (Eagle)
    - Ajolote (Axolotl)
    - Anaconda
    - Cangrejo (Crab)
    - Conejo (Rabbit)
    - Gato (Cat)
    - And many more!
    
    <Note>
      All avatars are displayed in a 4-column grid for easy browsing.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Favorite">
    Tap on any avatar to select it. The selected avatar will show:
    - A green border
    - A green checkmark badge
    - A light green background
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Selection">
    Your avatar will be immediately applied and saved. The modal will close automatically, and your new avatar will appear on your profile.
    
    <Warning>
      If you change your mind, simply tap **Cancelar** at the bottom of the modal to close without changing your avatar.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Viewing Your Curriculum

CUCEIUbicate includes access to your academic program's curriculum map:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Curriculum Card">
    On your profile screen, find the blue gradient card labeled **Malla Curricular** with your program name displayed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Curriculum Viewer">
    Tap anywhere on the card to open the curriculum viewer modal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View and Zoom">
    The curriculum image will display in full screen. You can:
    
    - **Pinch to zoom** in and out
    - **Double-tap** to zoom to a specific area
    - **Pan** to move around the zoomed image
    - **Swipe down** to close the viewer
    
    <Note>
      A hint appears at the bottom: "Pellizca para hacer zoom" (Pinch to zoom).
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Close the Viewer">
    Tap the **X** button in the top-right corner or the **Cerrar** button at the bottom to exit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  If your program's curriculum map is not available, you'll see a message: "Malla curricular no disponible" with information that it will be added soon.
</Warning>

## Understanding Your Profile Data

### What Information is Displayed?

Your profile displays information from your registration:

- **Name and Last Names**: As entered during profile completion
- **Username**: Unique identifier you chose (cannot be changed)
- **Student Code**: Your 9-digit CUCEI code (cannot be changed)
- **Degree Program**: Shows the full program name based on your selected degree code
- **Email**: Your registered institutional email

<Note>
  Your password is never displayed for security reasons. All sensitive information is encrypted and stored securely.
</Note>

### Available Degree Programs

CUCEIUbicate supports the following degree programs:

- ICIV - Ingeniería Civil
- IGFO - Ingeniería en Fotónica
- INBI - Ingeniería Biomédica
- INCE - Ingeniería en Comunicaciones y Electrónica
- INCO - Ingeniería en Computación
- INDU - Ingeniería Industrial
- INFO - Ingeniería en Informática
- INME - Ingeniería Mecánica Eléctrica
- INQU - Ingeniería Química
- INRO - Ingeniería en Robótica
- ITOG - Ingeniería en Topografía Geomática
- And more...

## Logging Out

To safely log out of your account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Logout Button">
    Scroll to the bottom of your profile screen to find the red **Cerrar Sesión** button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Logout">
    Tap the button. A confirmation dialog will appear asking: **¿Estás seguro de que quieres cerrar sesión?**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Complete Logout">
    - Tap **Sí, cerrar sesión** to log out and return to the login screen
    - Tap **Cancelar** to stay logged in
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Logging out will require you to enter your credentials again the next time you open the app.
</Warning>

## Profile Tips

### Choosing the Right Avatar

- Pick an avatar that represents your personality
- Animal avatars are fun and easy to recognize
- You can change your avatar anytime!

### Keeping Your Information Current

<Note>
  While most profile information cannot be edited after registration (username, student code, email), your avatar and status are always customizable.
</Note>

### Privacy and Security

- Your profile information is only visible to you
- Your password is encrypted and never displayed
- Always log out when using a shared device
