## Directory

The CUCEIUbicate directory provides quick access to contact information for academic and administrative personnel across the university.

### Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Search Contacts" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Easily search for contacts by name, position, or department
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contact Details" icon="address-card">
    Access phone extensions, email addresses, and office locations
  </Card>
  <Card title="Department Information" icon="building">
    Browse contacts organized by academic and administrative departments
  </Card>
  <Card title="Direct Actions" icon="phone">
    Call or email directly from the app with one tap
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### How to Use the Directory

1. **Open the Directory**: Navigate to the Community section and select Directory
2. **Search**: Use the search bar to find contacts by:
   - Name
   - Position/Title
   - Department
3. **View Details**: Each contact card displays:
   - Profile photo
   - Name and position
   - Department
   - Office location
   - Contact information
4. **Take Action**:
   - Tap the phone icon to call their extension
   - Tap the email button to send an email

<Note>
  The directory is regularly updated to ensure you have access to current contact information for all CUCEI faculty and staff.
</Note>

### Contact Information Includes

## Student Regulations (Reglamento Estudiantil)

Understand your rights and responsibilities as a CUCEI student by reviewing the student regulations available in the app.

### What You'll Find

The Articles section provides access to key articles from the General Regulations of the University of Guadalajara, specifically focusing on student rights, responsibilities, and conduct.

<Note type="warning">
  All students are expected to be familiar with and comply with the university's student regulations. These regulations govern academic conduct, student rights, and disciplinary procedures.
</Note>

### Key Articles Covered

The app provides easy access to important articles from the student regulations, organized in a clear, readable format. Each article includes:

- **Article Number**: Clear identification of each regulation
- **Subsections (Incisos)**: Detailed breakdowns of each article's provisions
- **Full Text**: Complete regulatory text for each article

<Accordion title="Why These Regulations Matter">
  The student regulations define:
  - Your academic rights and responsibilities
  - Standards of conduct expected on campus
  - Procedures for academic matters
  - Disciplinary processes and appeals
  - Student services and support available to you
</Accordion>

### Accessing the Full Document

While the app provides key articles for quick reference, you can access the complete official student regulations document:

1. **In-App Link**: Tap "Ver Reglamento Completo" (View Complete Regulations) or "Consultar Documento Oficial" (Consult Official Document)
2. **Official Source**: Opens the authoritative version hosted on the University of Guadalajara website
3. **Comprehensive Access**: View all articles, amendments, and supplementary regulations

<Card title="Official Document" icon="file-contract" href="#">
  Access the complete General Regulations of the University of Guadalajara through the in-app link
</Card>

### Important Information

<Note type="info">
  The regulations in the app are provided for informational purposes. Students should consult the official document for the most current and complete version of all university regulations.
</Note>

### How to Navigate Regulations

1. **Browse Articles**: Scroll through the available articles in the app
2. **Read Subsections**: Each article is broken down into clear subsections (incisos) for easy reading
3. **Search Topics**: Look for specific topics or keywords within the articles
4. **Access Full Document**: Use the link to view the complete official regulations when needed
