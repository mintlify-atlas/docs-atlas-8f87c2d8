## What is Radio CUCEI?

Radio CUCEI serves as both an educational platform and a campus-wide communication channel. The station is used for communications course practicals while functioning as a medium for broadcasting relevant university activities.

<Note>
  **Frequency:** 96.7 MHz FM  
  **Coverage:** CUCEI Campus Area  
  **Language:** Spanish  
  **Genre:** Varied
</Note>

## How to Listen

There are two ways to tune into Radio CUCEI:

### FM Radio
- **Frequency:** 96.7 MHz
- **Coverage:** Available throughout the CUCEI campus area
- Simply tune your FM radio to 96.7 MHz while on campus

### Online Streaming
- Visit the Radio CUCEI web player at [http://radio.cucei.udg.mx/reproductor.html](http://radio.cucei.udg.mx/reproductor.html)
- Listen from anywhere with internet access
- No installation required - works directly in your browser

## Purpose & Mission

Radio CUCEI was established with a dual purpose:

1. **Educational Platform**: Provides practical experience for students enrolled in communications courses, offering hands-on training in radio broadcasting, production, and content creation.

2. **Campus Communication**: Acts as a vital medium for disseminating important information about university activities, including:
   - Event announcements
   - Exam schedules and academic calendars
   - Cultural activities and entertainment programs
   - General campus news and updates

<Note type="info">
  Radio CUCEI is an experimental broadcasting system operated by students and faculty as part of the university's communications program.
</Note>
