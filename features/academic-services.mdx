## Absence Justification

Understand the policies and procedures for justifying class absences due to medical reasons.

### Important Policy Guidelines

<Note>
By university regulation, you can only justify 20% of absences for the entire semester.
</Note>

<Accordion title="What Absences Can Be Justified?">
  - Only the days marked on the medical prescription can be justified
  - Exception: IMSS disability certificates may cover additional days
  - Absences must be supported by medical documentation from a certified institution
</Accordion>

### Justification Process

1. **Obtain Medical Documentation**
   - Get a medical prescription from a certified institution (preferably IMSS)
   - Ensure the prescription clearly shows the dates requiring absence

2. **Submit to Your Coordinator**
   - Take the medical prescription to your academic coordinator
   - Coordinator will verify the information meets requirements

3. **Notification to Professors**
   - If approved, the justification will be sent to your professors
   - Delivery within 24 business hours after completing the process

<Note>
Only medical certificates from certified institutions like IMSS are accepted. Always submit documentation as soon as possible after your absence.
</Note>

## Document Requests

Request official academic documents through convenient self-service kiosks or online through SIATCE.

### Quick Service Kiosk

The fastest way to get common documents:

**Location**: In front of Control Escolar in Module A (same building as Proulex)

**Available Documents**:
- Scholarship certificates (Constancia para beca)
- Certified transcript (Kardex certificado)
- Certificate without grades (Constancia sin calificaciones)

<Note>
Document costs are charged to your student account. Payment is not required to receive the document - you only need the electronic or printed request receipt.
</Note>

### Online Requests via SIATCE

For additional document types, use the SIATCE system:

**Access**: http://escolar.cucei.udg.mx/cescolar/login.aspx

**Steps**:
1. Log in to SIATCE
2. Click "Solicitar algún documento" (Request a document)
3. Select the document type you need
4. Submit your request

### Available Documents

<Accordion title="Certified Copies">
  - Birth certificate (Copia certificada de acta de nacimiento)
  - High school certificate (Copia certificada del certificado de preparatoria)
  - Accreditation ruling copy (Copia dictamen de acreditación)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Academic Certificates">
  - Certificate with grades (Constancia con calificaciones)
  - Certificate with schedule (Constancia con horario)
  - Technical file certificate (Constancia de Ficha Técnica)
  - Certificate for scholarships (Constancia para beca)
  - Certificate without grades (Constancia sin calificaciones)
  - Certified transcript (Kardex certificado)
</Accordion>

### Important Information

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Payment" icon="money-bill">
    Charges are added to your account. Download the payment order one week after requesting. You can pay even after the due date expires.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Document Pickup" icon="clock">
    Documents are held at the window for one month after processing. After this period, they are destroyed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="No Prepayment Required" icon="check">
    You can pick up your document before paying. Just present your request receipt (electronic or printed).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Payment Orders

Manage tuition payments and fees through the LEO student platform.

### Checking Your Payment Order

**Access LEO**: https://leoalumnos.udg.mx

**Steps**:
1. Log in to your LEO account
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page
3. Click on "Orden de pago" (Payment order)
4. Download the payment format

### Payment Methods

<Accordion title="Bank Payment">
  - Take the downloaded format to any authorized bank
  - Pay at the teller window or ATM
  - Enter the agreement number (número de convenio) when prompted
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Payment Confirmation">
  - Payments take approximately 3 business days to reflect in the system
  - Once processed, your account will show $0 balance
  - Keep your payment receipt for your records
</Accordion>

### Payment Forgiveness

If you have a situation that prevents you from making full payment:

<Note>
Payment forgiveness (condonación) applications are available at the beginning of each semester.
</Note>

- Watch for announcements on your coordinator's official pages
- A form will be released at the start of the semester
- Submit your application according to the published guidelines

## Course Registration (Agenda)

Navigate the course pre-registration and registration process successfully.

### Step 1: Pre-Registration

<Note>
Pre-registration is mandatory to register courses in your designated period. Without it, you'll be assigned to General Registration after all other students.
</Note>

**Access LEO**: https://leoalumnos.udg.mx

**Process**:
1. Watch for pre-registration dates announced by your coordinator
2. Log in to LEO platform
3. Select "Mis cursos" (My courses)
4. Click "Pre-registro a cursos" (Course pre-registration)
5. Enter the course codes (claves) for classes you want to take

<Accordion title="Pre-Registration Periods">
  Your coordinator announces specific pre-registration dates for your program. Stay tuned to official communication channels:
  - Coordinator's website
  - Official social media accounts
  - Student email
  - Coordinator's office announcements
</Accordion>

### Step 2: Check Course Offerings

Before registration day, research available course sections:

**In LEO**:
1. Select "Malla curricular" (Curriculum)
2. Click "Oferta académica" (Academic offerings)
3. Choose "Centro Universitario de Ciencias Exactas e Ingenierías"
4. Select your degree program
5. Select the upcoming semester cycle
6. Enter the course code to view available sections
7. **Save the NRC codes** for sections that fit your schedule

<Accordion title="Understanding NRC Codes">
  NRC (Number of Reference Code) is a unique identifier for each course section. You'll need these codes to register for specific class times and professors during registration.
</Accordion>

### Step 3: Registration Day

**Preview Your Schedule**:
- Check "Mis cursos" → "Registro a cursos" 1-2 days before registration
- Your assigned registration time will be posted by your coordinator

**During Registration**:
1. Log in to LEO at your assigned time
2. Navigate to "Mis cursos" → "Registro a cursos"
3. Enter the NRC codes for your desired courses
4. Submit your registration

<Note>
If an NRC code shows an error, remove it and try a different available section. Errors typically mean the section is full or has a schedule conflict.
</Note>

### Registration Tips

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Be Prepared" icon="list-check">
    Have multiple NRC options for each course in case your first choice is full.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Log In Early" icon="clock">
    Access the system a few minutes before your registration time to avoid technical issues.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Prioritize Courses" icon="star">
    Register for required courses first, then electives if time remains.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Check Prerequisites" icon="book">
    Ensure you've completed all prerequisite courses before attempting to register.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
