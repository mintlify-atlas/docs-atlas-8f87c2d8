## Overview

CUCEI has implemented a facial recognition system at all campus entry points to enhance security while providing convenient access for students, faculty, and staff. This system allows for contactless, quick entry to campus facilities.

<Note type="warning">
  You must register your face in the system to access CUCEI facilities. Without registration, you may be unable to enter campus buildings.
</Note>

## Registration Process

Follow these steps to register your face in the CUCEI access system:

### Step 1: Access the Registration Portal

<Card title="Go to Registration Portal" icon="arrow-up-right-from-square" href="https://acceso.cucei.udg.mx">
  Visit https://acceso.cucei.udg.mx to begin registration
</Card>

Navigate to the CUCEI facial recognition registration website at [https://acceso.cucei.udg.mx](https://acceso.cucei.udg.mx)

### Step 2: Select Your Category

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Students" icon="user-graduate">
    Choose this option if you are a currently enrolled CUCEI student
  </Card>
  <Card title="Faculty/Staff" icon="chalkboard-user">
    Select this if you are academic or administrative personnel
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

1. In the "COMUNIDAD CUCEI" section, click the "Subir Foto" (Upload Photo) button
2. Choose your category:
   - **Estudiante** (Student)
   - **Trabajador académico/administrativo** (Academic/Administrative worker)

### Step 3: Login with Your Credentials

Enter your SIIAU credentials:
- **Código** (Code): Your student or employee code
- **NIP**: Your SIIAU password

<Note>
  Use the same credentials you use to access SIIAU (Sistema de Información y Administración Universitaria).
</Note>

### Step 4: Upload Your Photo

<Accordion title="Photo Requirements & Best Practices">
  To ensure successful facial recognition:
  
  - **Face the camera directly** with good lighting
  - **Remove glasses** if possible (or ensure no glare)
  - **Neutral expression** with eyes open
  - **No hats or accessories** covering your face
  - **Plain background** when possible
  - **Current photo** that accurately represents your appearance
  - **High quality** image without blur or pixelation
</Accordion>

1. Click "Subir foto" (Upload photo)
2. Follow the on-screen recommendations for taking a good photo
3. Take your photo or select one from your device
4. Press "Guardar" (Save) to confirm your photo

### Step 5: Add Email (Optional)

You can optionally provide your email address for notifications and updates about the access system.

### Step 6: Complete Registration

Click "Guardar datos" (Save data) to finalize your registration. Your photo will be processed and added to the facial recognition system.

<Note type="success">
  After successful registration, allow up to 24 hours for your face to be fully processed in the access system.
</Note>

## Using the Facial Recognition System

Once registered, using the system is simple:

1. **Approach the access point**: Walk up to any CUCEI entrance with facial recognition
2. **Face the camera**: Look directly at the camera device
3. **Wait for recognition**: The system will identify you within seconds
4. **Access granted**: The door will unlock automatically when you're recognized

<Card title="Access Points" icon="door-open">
  Facial recognition is available at all main CUCEI campus entry points, providing secure 24/7 access for authorized personnel.
</Card>

## Troubleshooting

### Photo Issues

If you need to change or update your photo due to recognition problems:

<Accordion title="In-Person Photo Update">
  **Location:** Entrance "Revolución"  
  **Hours:** 7:00 AM - 5:00 PM  
  **Who to see:** Student service volunteers (prestadores de servicio social)
  
  Visit the Revolución entrance during operating hours. Student volunteers can help you retake your photo and update it in the system.
</Accordion>

### System Problems

If you experience issues with the facial recognition system:

<Accordion title="Contact Security Coordination">
  **Office:** Coordinación de Seguridad y Protección Universitaria  
  **Location:** Rectoría building  
  **Hours:** 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM  
  
  For technical problems or access issues, visit the Security and University Protection Coordination office located in the Rectoría building.
</Accordion>

<Note type="warning">
  If you cannot access campus due to system issues, contact the Security Coordination office immediately or seek assistance at the nearest staffed entrance.
</Note>

## Common Issues & Solutions

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Not Recognized" icon="face-frown">
    **Solution:** Ensure good lighting, face the camera directly, and remove sunglasses. If issues persist, update your photo.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Changed Appearance" icon="user-pen">
    **Solution:** If you've significantly changed your appearance (haircut, facial hair, etc.), update your photo at the Revolución entrance.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Slow Recognition" icon="clock">
    **Solution:** Approach the camera at a normal pace and face it directly. Avoid quick movements.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Access Denied" icon="ban">
    **Solution:** Verify your registration is complete and processed. Contact Security Coordination if you've waited more than 24 hours after registration.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
