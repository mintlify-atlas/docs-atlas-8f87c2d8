## What is CUCEIUbicate?

CUCEIUbicate is a comprehensive mobile application designed specifically for students at CUCEI (Centro Universitario de Ciencias Exactas e Ingenierías). The app provides an interactive campus navigation system, AI-powered assistance, and seamless access to essential student services—all in one place.

## Key Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Real-time Navigation" icon="route">

## Built for Students

CUCEIUbicate was developed by students, for students. We understand the challenges of navigating a large university campus and the frustration of accessing scattered services. Our mission is to make campus life easier, more efficient, and more connected.

<Note>
  CUCEIUbicate is an open-source project maintained by the iLabTDI team. We welcome contributions from the community!
